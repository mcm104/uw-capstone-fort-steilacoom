Eliza C. Porter

Alternate names

  • Eliza Porter
  • Mrs. Porter

Biography

First public school teacher in Chicago, at Fort Dearborn, established normal schools, educated settlers and American Indians at Mackinac Island, aided the wounded during the American Civil War as a member of the U.S. Sanitary Commission, participated in the Underground Railroad, and taught freedmen.
