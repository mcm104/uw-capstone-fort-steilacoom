Stikine Tlingit

Definition

The Stikine people are a ḵwáan or regional group of the Tlingit, indigenous peoples of the Pacific Northwest Coast of North America. Their historical territory included Wrangell Island and other islands of the Alexander Archipelago, as well as the basin of the lower Stikine River.
