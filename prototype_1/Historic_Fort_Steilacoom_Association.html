Historic Fort Steilacoom Association (1983-present)

Definition

Established in 1983, with the purpose of restoring and preserving historic Fort Steilacoom in order to perpetuate the significance of the site for the benefit of the public by operating a museum complex and a strong educational program stressing the role of the Fort in Northwest history.
