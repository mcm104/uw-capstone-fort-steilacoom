Fort Steilacoom (1849-1868)

Definition

Founded by the U.S. Army in 1849 near Lake Steilacoom, among the first military fortifications built by the U.S. north of the Columbia River in what was to become the State of Washington, the fort was constructed due to civilian agitation about the massacre in 1847 at the Whitman (Waiilatpu) mission.
